Contributing ​

JustRelax.jl is an open-source project and we are very happy to accept contributions from the community. Please feel free to open Issues (issue templeates in here) or submit Pull Requests (PR templeate in here) to the main branch with your contribution. For planned large contributions, it is often beneficial to get in contact with one of the principal developers first (see Authors).
